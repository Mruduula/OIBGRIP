Sudha Murthy
(Indian Educator)

Sudha Murty born 19 August 1951, is an Indian educator, author and philanthropist who is chairperson of the Infosys Foundation.
Sudha Murty began her professional career in computer science and engineering.
She is a member of the public health care initiatives of the Gates Foundation.
She is best known for her philanthropy and her literature contribution in Kannada and English

Personal life
Sudha Murty married N. R. Narayana Murthy while employed as an engineer at TELCO in Pune.
The couple have two children, including fashion designer Akshata Murty and Indian Executive Assistant Rohan Murthy
